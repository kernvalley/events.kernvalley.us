# Empowering Our Community: The KVHD Health Fair

Geographical isolation and limited transportation often create significant hurdles for families in the Kern River Valley. To ensure our community has the support it needs, the Kern Valley Healthcare District (KVHD) is hosting a free Community Health Fair on the hospital grounds.

This event is dedicated to connecting residents with vital healthcare screenings, education, and preventative resources. We are committed to bringing essential health services directly to our neighbors, empowering them with the tools and information necessary to live healthier, more vibrant lives.

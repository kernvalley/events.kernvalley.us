## Happy Earth Day - You are invited! 

Join us this Saturday, April 25th at 8:00 AM as we come together to plant seedlings and grow something meaningful for our community. 🌿

Help us provide fresh, healthy vegetables for those in need while connecting with neighbors and making a real impact. This is more than planting—it’s about cultivating a stronger, more caring community.

- ✨ Meet the UC Master Gardener Program Coordinator
- ✨ Connect with our dedicated Garden Leaders
- ✨ Be part of a space that opens EVERY WEEK to those ready to learn, grow, and give back

Whether you’re an experienced gardener or just curious to get your hands in the soil, everyone is welcome. Come share in the learning, laughter, and joy of growing together.

Let’s plant the seeds for the future we want to see in our valley—neighbors helping neighbors. 💚

**🌼 Ways to Help:**
- Share this post on your page or in your groups
- Invite friends and family to join
- Spread the word in community groups
- Show up—many hands make great work!
